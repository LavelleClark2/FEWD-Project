Characters

The base roster includes 21 playable characters, with 3 additional characters being unlockable through gameplay and a further 19 being released as paid downloadable content (DLC) via a series of "FighterZ Passes" for a total of 43. Android 21 is a new character original to the game, designed by series creator Akira Toriyama.
